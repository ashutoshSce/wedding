About Us

Proposal

It was Valentines Day, so I was sort of expecting a special night, but I had no idea how special! I should begin by saying that Brandon and I had talked about marriage in the past, but I was convinced he wasn't ready yet to take the plunge! I knew he loved me, but was I ever shocked when he proposed!

We had plans to go out to dinner at a new restaurant I'd been dying to try. They had a special menu for Valentines Day which included one red rose between every course (a bit of bribery couldn't hurt the chances I'd say yes, right?). The last course was chocolate cheesecake for two, shaped like a heart. As the server set it between us on the table, the restaurant's violinist came over to the table playing "Here Comes the Bride". I was totally confused for a minute, until I saw the ring hidden in the strawberry topping of the cheesecake. Then I looked at Brandon, who was getting down on one knee and grinning from ear to ear. Realizing (duh!) what was going on, I burst into tears of joy before he could even say anything! We debate to this day whether he actually asked the question (I'm sure he did, but he insists I said yes before he even had time to ask). Either way, we're getting married!

About Us

Our Story

Brandon's Version:

As part of my residency, I work in the ER at the University of Chicago Medical Center. I was treating a young boy for minor injuries after being in a car accident with his parents, who were both in more critical condition. After treating him, he was still in shock and very scared, so I brought him over to the psychiatric department so someone could take care of him until family members arrived. When I walked in, there was Amanda, whom I had seen around the hospital (it's hard to miss such a beauty!) but hadn't really ever spoken to. She was so great with him! Later that day I went back to follow up - and ask her out! I guess you could say the rest is history.

Amanda's Version:

I had had my eye on Brandon for a while (who wouldn't!), but had never gotten up the nerve to talk to him. When he walked in my department that day with that poor little boy, I was so nervous! But it turned out that he was just so sweet and kind that he put me right at ease. Later when he came back and asked me out, I was totally floored! Of course I said yes, and a few days later we went on our first of many, many dates!

P.S. both parents and the little boy were fine.
